The guide's name

This guide is not an official guide and is not written by Moog Music, so it needed another name than 'Animoog Guide' to remove confusion about its source (and avoid eventual legal imbroglio!).

As initiator of this guide and main contributor, it has been named 'Satri Animoog Guide'. Satri is my pseudonym under which I published a free album of improvised Animoog solos. I am also the initiator and maintainer of Animoog.org. I am open to changing the name of the guide, share your suggestions here.
